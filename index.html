About Us

Scrap into wealth, making a difference in the world, one trade at a time.

A scrap trading company with 17 years of industry experience, Shreeji Traders is a well-established and reliable business that has been serving the community for a long time. Established by Mr. Sanjaykumar Rambhai Patel, the firm has developed an in-depth understanding of the scrap trading industry, as well as a vast network of suppliers, customers, and partners over time. With such a wealth of knowledge and experience, the organisation has become a trusted source for scrap materials and its trading, offering customers high-quality products and excellent customer service.
